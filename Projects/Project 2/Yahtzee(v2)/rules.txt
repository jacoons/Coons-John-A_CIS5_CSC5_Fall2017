

RULES: The game consists of 13 rounds. Each player rolls five dice and
attempts to score different combinations. Each turn consists of 3 rolls.
A round is complete when each player has taken their turn. For each
roll, the player may choose to keep any number of dice he or she wishes.
The object of the game is to fill every possible scoring combination,
and the player with the highest total score wins! For each round, each
player must enter a score into one of the combinations. The player may
enter a 0 into any box if he or she does not wish to enter a score for
the current dice combination left at the end of the turn.


              	       Good luck, and have fun!!!
